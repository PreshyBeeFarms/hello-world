Who We Are

Feeding Families. Supporting Communities.

PreshyBee Farms Group is a Lagos-based integrated agribusiness. From our poultry houses to snail pens, goat paddocks to pepper greenhouses — every product leaves our farm with a promise: uncompromising quality at prices that respect your pocket.

Our mission is simple: to make protein and fresh food available for the common masses. We believe that good nutrition should not be a luxury — it should be accessible to every Nigerian household.
